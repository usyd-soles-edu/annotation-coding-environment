Detailed metrics

How verdicts are computed. Status is based on Gwet's AC1, which simulation studies show tracks true reliability more accurately than alpha or kappa under the prevalence conditions typical of text annotation (Zhao et al., 2022). Thresholds: Reliable ≥ 0.80, Tentative 0.60–0.79, Unreliable < 0.60. When alpha appears low despite high agreement, ACE checks for the prevalence paradox (Feinstein & Cicchetti, 1990) and flags it in the per-code guidance. For full details including threshold derivation and paradox detection algorithm, download the methodology document from the export buttons at the top of the page.
